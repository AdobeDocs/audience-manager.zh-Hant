# Manage Data Feed Subscriptions {#manage-data-feed-subscriptions}

The [!UICONTROL Marketplace] is where data buyers go to research and subscribe to public and private data feeds. 請依照下列步驟訂閱公開資料饋送。

## Subscribe to a Public Data Feed {#subscript-public-data-feed}

The [!UICONTROL Marketplace] is where data buyers go to research and subscribe to public and private data feeds. 請依照下列步驟訂閱公開資料饋送。

<!-- t_subscribe_feed.xml -->

若要訂閱公開資料饋送：

1. **[!UICONTROL Audience Marketplace > Marketplace]**&#x200B;前往。使用搜尋功能或瀏覽清單以尋找資料饋送。

   ![](assets/subscribe1.png)

1. 按一下您要使用的資料饋送名稱。This opens the [plan details page](../../../features/audience-marketplace/marketplace-data-buyers/marketplace-manage-subscriptions.md#marketplace-buyer-details) for the selected feed.

   ![](assets/plan-details.png)

1. 從訂閱表格中選擇使用案例，然後：
   * Move the **[!UICONTROL Subscription]** slider to **[!UICONTROL On]**.
   * Click **[!UICONTROL Review & Subscribe]**. This opens the [!UICONTROL Terms and Conditions] window.
   ![](assets/subscribe3.png)

1. In the [!UICONTROL Terms and Conditions] window:

   * **重要：** 勾選 **[!UICONTROL ID sync]** 核取方塊。此設定有助於改善資料供應商的匹配率。
   * Check the terms and conditions box and click **[!UICONTROL Accept]** to complete the subscription process.
   ![](assets/subscribe4.png)

### 後續步驟

訂閱資料饋送後：

* Verify the subscription by checking your [!UICONTROL Traits] folder. See [Storage for Subscribed Data Feeds](../../../features/audience-marketplace/marketplace-data-buyers/marketplace-manage-subscriptions.md#find-subscribed-data-fee).

* 檢閱帳單和付款文件。請參閱以下相關連結。

>

## Subscribe to a Private Data Feed {#subscript-private-data-feed}

Buyers subscribe to private data feeds and plans in **[!UICONTROL Audience Marketplace > Marketplace]**.

<!-- t_private_feed.xml -->

>[!TIP]
>
>有時候資料供應商可能會對私人資料饋送提供折扣。提交訂閱申請時，您可能想要詢問可能的折扣。

訂閱私人資料饋送：

1. Click the data feed name in the [!UICONTROL Marketplace].
1. Click **[!UICONTROL Request Access]**. 如此會開啓請求對話方塊。
1. In the request dialog box, write the provider a note expressing your interest in their data feed and click **[!UICONTROL Send]**. 賣方會檢閱您的訊息，並核准或拒絕您的要求。While waiting for approval, "Requested" appears in the [!UICONTROL Marketplace] list for that data feed.

   * **[!UICONTROL Request approved]**： [!UICONTROL Marketplace] 清單中的狀態會變更為「授予的存取權」，您將會收到自動通知。此時您可以訂閱動態消息。See [Subscribe to a Public Data Feed](../../../features/audience-marketplace/marketplace-data-buyers/marketplace-manage-subscriptions.md#subscript-public-data-feed) for instructions.
   * **[!UICONTROL Request denied]**：「請求的」文字會從動態消息 [!UICONTROL Marketplace] 的清單中移除。您可以嘗試再次訂閱或選擇不同的動態消息。

## Data Feed Deactivation: Why It Happens and How to Respond {#data-feed-deactivation-reasons}

In [!UICONTROL Audience Marketplace], data providers can revoke access to your subscribed data feeds. 如果發生這種情況，請勿暫停。我們提供您所需的一切。請參閱本節有關資料饋送停用程序與程序的章節。

## Common Reasons for Data Feed Deactivation {#reasons-for-deactivation}

<!-- marketplace-subscriber-deactivated.xml -->

如果您訂閱的動態消息關閉，可能會是拼湊或甚至更新。不過，資料供應商可基於多種原因停用資料饋送。常見原因包括：

* **帳單：** 如果您拖欠費用或未支付費用，資料供應商將會停用動態消息。
* **動態消息更新：** 資料供應商在更新動態消息分類或成本結構時，必須停用動態消息。
* **非活動中購買者：** 如果訂閱者未在延長期間顯示任何支出，資料供應商保留停用動態消息的權利。
* **非活動中銷售者：** 離開的 [!UICONTROL Audience Marketplace] 資料提供者將停用並刪除其所有資料饋送。

>[!TIP]
>
>如果您認為資料饋送意外停用，請直接連絡資料提供者。[!DNL Adobe] 您的顧問可以協助您取得聯絡資訊或其他支援。

## Deactivation Email {#deactivation-email}

When a data provider deactivates one of your data feeds, [!DNL Audience Manager] sends an email to the users in your company who have [!UICONTROL Administrator] permissions. 有時候，電子郵件篩選器會將此訊息歸類為垃圾訊息。因此，您可能會錯過此重要通知。為了協助您識別停用訊息，此電子郵件包含下列元素：

* **寄件者：** 停用電子郵件來自 `aam-noreply@adobe.com`。Pro提示：請勿回覆本電子郵件。

* **主旨列：** 此處的資料饋送 *名稱* 已取消。

* **附件：** 電子郵件包含附加標題」 `list-of-affected-entities-by-feed-revocation.csv`。這表示附件會列出已取消動態消息中包含的所有特性。身為資料購買者，您應檢閱此附件。It will help you find and remove deactivated traits from your segments and [algorithmic models](../../../features/algorithmic-models/understanding-models.md).

## Remove Deactivated Traits {#remove-deactivated-traits}

身為資料購買者，您有責任從所有作用中/非活動中區段中移除已取消動態消息中的特性。移除選項包括：

* Bulk removal with the [REST APIs](../../../api/rest-api-main/rest-api-main.md) or the [Bulk Management Tools](../../../reference/bulk-management-tools/bulk-management-intro.md).

* Manually search for affected segments and remove deactivated traits using [!UICONTROL Segment Builder]. See [Remove Traits from a Segment](../../../features/segments/segment-builder.md#segment-builder-controls-traits).

>[!NOTE]
>
>從作用中演算法模型或目的地移除特徵會影響縮放和定位準確度。盡可能嘗試取代已撤銷的特性，並使用新的有效特性。

[從帳戶移除所有已撤銷的特性](../../../features/audience-marketplace/marketplace-data-buyers/marketplace-manage-subscriptions.md#unsubscribe) 後，取消訂閱已停用的資料饋送。如果這是暫時停用，您可以在資料提供者完成必要變更並重新啓動動態消息後重新訂閱。As with most things, good communication with your partners (the data provider and [!DNL Adobe]) can help you work through this process.

## Understanding Data Feed Use Cases {#use-cases}

[!UICONTROL Audience Marketplace] 身為資料購買者，您可以購買重疊、模型和啓動使用案例的資料。每個使用案例都是為了特定用途而設計，並限制您對資料的處理方式。這些使用案例說明可協助您決定要購買哪種類型的資料計劃。

## Make Comparisons with Segments and Overlap Plans {#comparisons}

<!-- c_use_cases_for_buyers.xml -->

### 區段和重疊

This use case lets you compare your traits with provider traits in a [trait-to-trait overlap report.](../../../reporting/dynamic-reports/trait-trait-overlap-report.md#trait-to-trait-overlap-report) 此外，您也可以建立或新增提供者特徵至區段，並與 [區段對特徵](../../../reporting/dynamic-reports/segment-trait-overlap-report.md) 和 [區段對區段](../../../reporting/dynamic-reports/segment-segment-overlap-report.md) 報表進行其他比較。重疊比較可協助您：

* **擴大受眾覆蓋面：** 低重疊表示您的特徵包含您之前未曾見過的使用者。您可能希望這些特性能試用新使用者。
* **增強現有受眾：** 重疊表示您的特徵類似資料提供者所擁有的特性。您可能希望這些特性能夠為已開發的受眾帶來有針對性的漸進式改進。

### 演算法模型

This use case lets you evaluate supplier traits against your traits with [algorithmic modeling](../../../features/algorithmic-models/understanding-models.md#understanding-models). 例如，我們的演算模型系統會使用您的一個特徵作為比較供應商特徵的基礎。當模型執行時，可以顯示供應商特徵中的對象是否與您的特徵共用相似的轉換屬性。

### 啟用

This use case lets you send data to a [destination](../../../features/destinations/destinations.md). In [!UICONTROL Audience Manager], a destination is any third-party system (ad server, [!DNL DSP], [!DNL DMP], exchange, etc.) 您要與其共用資料。However, with an [!UICONTROL Activation] use case, you cannot run overlap reports or test the data in an algorithmic model.
